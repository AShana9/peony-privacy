Peony — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026  ·  Effective: 21 June 2026

Peony ("we", "us", the "app") helps you understand your menstrual cycle, symptoms, mood and wellbeing. We know this is some of the most personal information there is, and we treat it that way. This policy explains what we collect, how it is used, and the control you have over it.

The short version: Your health data stays on your device by default. We do not sell it, we do not share it with third parties, and you can export or delete everything at any time.

1. Who we are

Peony is provided by Peony. If you have any questions about this policy or your data, contact us at privacyfirst.app@gmail.com.

2. What data Peony handles

  • Cycle & health data you enter: period dates, cycle and symptom logs, moods, notes, and life-stage settings (e.g. pregnancy, perimenopause).
  • App preferences: settings such as reminder choices, tone and appearance.
  • Optional account (only if you create one): email address, used solely to sync your data across your own devices.

3. Where your data is stored

Local-first by design. Unless you explicitly turn on optional cloud sync, everything you log is stored only on your device. We practise data minimisation — we collect only what the app needs to work for you.

4. How your data is used

Your data is used only to provide the app's features to you — showing your cycle, predictions, insights, reminders and educational content. We do not use your health data for advertising, and we do not sell or rent your personal data to anyone.

5. Sharing

  • We do not share your personal or health data with third parties for their own purposes.
  • Partner sharing (if you choose to use it) shares only the specific information you select, only with the person you invite.
  • Advertising (free tier): if/when ads are shown, they are served without using your health data, and we apply strict ad-content standards. [Update this section before enabling ads.]

6. Your rights and choices

  • Access & export: export your data (e.g. PDF/CSV) at any time.
  • Delete: delete individual entries or wipe all of your data from within the app.
  • GDPR / UK GDPR / CCPA: where applicable, you have rights to access, correct, delete, and port your data, and to object to or restrict processing. Contact us to exercise them.

7. Reproductive-health data & legal requests

We recognise that menstrual and reproductive data can be sensitive in some jurisdictions. Because Peony keeps your data on your device by default and minimises what is collected, there is no central store of your reproductive data for us to disclose. If we ever receive a legal request, we will only ever be able to provide data we actually hold.

8. Medical disclaimer

Peony is a wellness and education tool, not a medical device. Predictions can be wrong, and Peony is not a contraceptive — use protection any time you are avoiding pregnancy. Peony does not diagnose conditions; it surfaces patterns and may suggest you speak with a doctor.

9. Children

Peony is intended for users aged 16 and over and is not directed at children under that age.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including an optional app lock (PIN / biometric). No method of storage or transmission is ever 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, notify you in-app.

12. Contact

Questions or requests: privacyfirst.app@gmail.com.
